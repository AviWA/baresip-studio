baresip Privacy Policy

baresip application does not collect any personal information.

baresip needs access to microphone for the purpose of voice communication with other parties.

It can be chosen in Settings that baresip is using Android contacts as references to SIP and tel URIs.  If so, baresip app does not store Android contacts nor share them with anyone.

baresip supports secure call setup and messaging via TLS and secure media via ZRTP and (DTLS) SRTP encapsulation options.
