baresip+ Privacy Policy

baresip+ does not collect any personal information.

baresip+ needs access to microphone and camera for the purpose of voice
and video communication with other parties.

User can choose in Settings that baresip+ is using Android contacts for calling and messaging. If so, contact information is only used inside the application while baresip+ is running and is not leaked outside of the application.

baresip+ supports secure call setup via TLS and secure media via
ZRTP and (DTLS) SRTP encapsulation options.
