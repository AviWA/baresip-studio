baresip+ Privacy Policy

baresip+ does not collect any personal or app usage information nor share such information with anyone.

baresip+ needs access to microphone and camera for the purpose of voice and video communication with other parties.

It can be optionally chosen in Settings that baresip+ uses Android contacts as references to SIP and tel URIs.  If chosen, baresip+ does not store Android contacts nor share them with anyone.

baresip+ supports secure call setup and messaging via TLS and secure media via ZRTP and (DTLS) SRTP encapsulation options.
